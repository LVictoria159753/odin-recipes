Vanilla Pudding

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • A pinch of salt

  1. Put all the ingredients except the butter and vanilla extract in a saucepan
  2. Mix all ingredients and place over medium heat. Stir the mixutre over the flame until thickened
  3. Take off the heat and add the butter and vanilla extract
  4. Optional: strain mixture to remove lumps
  5. Transfer the pudding into another container and chill. Refridgerate for several hours and enjoy!
