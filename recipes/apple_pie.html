Apple Pie

Make a delicious homemade apple pie!

Ingredients

  • 6 apples
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 cups flour

Steps

  1. Cut the butter into flour. Mix until you get pea sized butter lumps in the flour.
  2. Add yogurt into flour and butter mixture. Refrigerate after forming a dough
  3. Cut apples into slices
  4. Combine,the apple slices and sugar together and place into a saucepan
  5. Cook mixture into thickened or when the sugar coats the back of your spoon.
  6. Roll flour out into two large circles. Put one part of the dough into a pie pan
  7. Add the filling and seal the pie up with the other circle of dough
  8. Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes or until golden brown
